U-wind at 10m from eastward wind
================================

* **Description**: produces dry surface eastward wind at 10m using eastward wind at the lowest atmospheric level
* **Name**: uwind_at_10m_A
* **Variable produced**: uwind_at_10m
* **Input Variables**: eastward_wind
* **Number of Levels** - 1
* **FunctionSpace** - The same FunctionSpace as the input eastward wind Field
* **hasTLAD** - True
* **Optional Parameters**: None
